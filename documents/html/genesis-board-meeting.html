S.E.E Genesis Board Meeting

Vision

To be a leading youth development organization, recognized for our innovative programs, passionate team, and commitment to empowering young people. We envision a future where all young people have access to opportunities, resources, and support to thrive and reach their full potential.

Mission

To provide a supportive and inclusive environment where young people can develop their skills, build their confidence, and unlock their full potential. We aim to produce a youth enriched with faith and full of the Word of God. We strive to foster a sense of belonging, promote social responsibility, and encourage young people to become active contributors to their communities.

Slogan and Logo

  • Slogan: "Ye are the light of the world, a chosen generation."
  • Logo: Features an eagle, symbolizing majestic and incomparable qualities.

Goals of S.E.E.

Short-Term Goals:

  • Assist with NSFAS applications.
  • Facilitate CAO applications.
  • Guide TVET College applications.
  • Organize exam prayer sessions.
  • Provide attainable "Exam Care Packages" for learners.

Long-Term Goals:

  • Host academic camps with qualified tutors.
  • Ensure safe transportation for learners attending academic camps.
  • Implement efficient cross-night programs.
  • Offer a Computer/Digital Literacy Program.
  • Organize sports tournaments.
  • Introduce learnership opportunities.

Important Reviews

  • Organization registration.
  • Establishment of a domain.
  • Update of contact details.
  • Physical and postal address documentation.
  • Opening a company bank account.
  • Securing sponsors.
  • Development of a website and management of social media accounts.
