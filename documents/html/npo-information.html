Vision

"To be a leading youth development organization, recognized for our innovative programs, passionate team, and commitment to empowering young people. We envision a future where all young people have access to opportunities, resources, and support to thrive and reach their full potential."

Mission

"To provide a supportive and inclusive environment where young people can develop their skills, build their confidence, and unlock their full potential. We strive to foster a sense of belonging, promote social responsibility, and encourage young people to become active contributors to their communities."

Slogan: "Ye are the light of the world, a chosen generation."

Logo: The eagle, symbolizing majestic and incomparable qualities.

Short-Term Goals

  • NSFAS applications
  • CAO applications
  • TVET College applications
  • Exam prayer sessions with "Exam Care Packages" for learners

Long-Term Goals

  • Academic camps with tutors
  • Safe transportation of learners
  • Cross-night programs
  • Computer/Digital Literacy Programs
  • Sports tournaments
  • Learnerships

Rules of S.E.E

  • Respect and discipline are mandatory
  • No profanity
  • No romantic relationships between board members
  • Non-compliance allows for three warnings before dismissal
  • Monthly membership fees are compulsory
  • Misuse of authority is prohibited
  • Members must provide prior notice of unavailability for online meetings
  • Transparency is required; no secret dealings allowed
  • Gossiping and bad-mouthing are prohibited

Christian Foundation: S.E.E. is grounded in Christianity, putting God at the forefront of all its endeavors.

Message: We are the youth and future of Sandanezwe. Through dedication and innovation, we aim to uplift and empower our community.

Membership Fee: R50 per month (generous contributions are welcome).

Payment Deadline: Payable from the 1st to the 5th of each month.
