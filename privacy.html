Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your information

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Sandanezwe Embodiment of Excellence (S.E.E) is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our website visitors, participants, and stakeholders in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information submitted through our contact form (name, email, message)
  • Information voluntarily shared for program participation
  • Basic analytics data about website usage

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used for:

  • Responding to your inquiries and messages
  • Providing information about our programs and services
  • Improving our website and user experience
  • Communicating important updates about our organization

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your web browser preferences.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external resources (bursary sites, university websites, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their respective privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights under POPIA, contact us at info@sandanezweexcellence.co.za.
