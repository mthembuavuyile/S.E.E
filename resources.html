Study Tips & Strategies

  • Create a realistic study timetable and stick to it
  • Practice with past papers under timed conditions
  • Use mind maps and summaries for revision
  • Take regular breaks — try the Pomodoro Technique
  • Join study groups or find a study buddy
  • Keep track of your progress in each subject
